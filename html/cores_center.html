CORES Center

Led by Salvadorans with a strong commitment to the leadership, development, and empowerment of their community, the CORES (Committee on Refugees from El Salvador) Center is located at 343 Medford Street, Somerville, MA 02145. The center is open Monday - Friday 9am - 5pm and Saturday 10am - 3pm.

El Centro de Refugiados de El Salvador fue organizado por Salvadoreños con un fuerte compromiso al liderazgo, desarrollo, y empoderamiento.
